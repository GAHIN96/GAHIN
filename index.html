About Mehmat

Institute of Mathematics, Mechanics and Computer Sciences

The Largest Institute of Natural Sciences in the Southern Federal University

The Institute of Mathematics, Mechanics and Computer Sciences (Mehmat) is the largest institute of natural sciences in the Southern Federal University. Until 2006 it was called the Faculty of Mechanics and Mathematics, but its name was changed to reflect its expansion and the opening of the "Fundamental Informatics and Information Technology" direction.

  • Full Range of Directions and Specialties

    Mathematics, Applied Mathematics and Computer Science, Mechanics and Mathematical Modeling, Fundamental Computer Science and Information Technology, Information Systems and Technologies

  • Highly Qualified Teaching Staff

    150 teachers, including 40 doctors of sciences and professors, 94 candidates of sciences and associate professors. Honored Scientists of Russia, Soros professors, and Soros associate professors are among them.

  • Modern Computer Base

    12 computer classes, powerful computing clusters, and a single computer network connecting departments and laboratories

  • Academic Building with Necessary Conditions

    Multimedia classrooms, reading room, sports hall, student cafe, lecture hall with audio and video equipment for scientific conferences and concerts, exhibitions of student works

  • Graduates with Wide Profile

    Specialists in mathematics and its applications, computer science and information technology, working in science, education, high and computer technologies, and fields subject to informatization.
